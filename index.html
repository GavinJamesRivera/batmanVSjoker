Instructions

Use arrow keys or WASD to move Batman.
Catch Joker for 100 points.
Collect purple squares (+15s time).
Collect red squares (+1 life).
Avoid orange bombs (starting level 4).
You have 3 lives. Good luck!

Level Guide

Level 1: Slow Joker, no bombs.
Level 2: Faster Joker, no bombs.
Level 3: Max Joker speed, no bombs.
Level 4+: Max Joker speed + bombs dropped by Joker!
